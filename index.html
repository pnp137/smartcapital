About Our Short-term Business Loans

Our short-term business loans are specifically designed for MSMEs who need quick access to working capital. Unlike traditional loans with fixed EMIs, our merchant cash advance model allows you to repay based on your daily sales, making it perfect for businesses with fluctuating cash flows. Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our business loans

What is a Merchant Cash Advance?

A Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) is a financing option where you receive an upfront sum in exchange for a percentage of your future digital sales. Unlike traditional loans, repayment is tied to your daily sales volume, making it flexible for businesses with variable income.

How quickly can I get the funds?

Once approved, funds are typically disbursed within 24-48 hours. Our streamlined process and minimal documentation requirements ensure quick access to working capital when you need it most.

What if my daily sales are low on some days?

That's the beauty of our model! On days with lower sales, your repayment amount automatically adjusts downward. You only pay a percentage of what you actually earn, ensuring cash flow is never strained.

Do I need collateral or guarantors?

No, our merchant cash advance is unsecured. We don't require collateral or personal guarantors. The advance is secured against your future digital sales receivables.

What digital payment systems do you integrate with?

We integrate with all major payment gateways including Razorpay, PayU, CCAvenue, and POS systems from major providers. Our team will help set up the integration seamlessly.

Can I repay early without penalties?

Yes, early repayment is allowed without any prepayment penalties. This can help you save on the total cost of the advance.

What is the minimum CIBIL score required?

We require a minimum CIBIL score of 650. However, we also consider other factors like business performance, cash flow patterns, and digital payment history in our assessment.

Are there any hidden charges?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All costs including the factor rate and processing fee are clearly disclosed upfront. There are no hidden charges or surprise fees.

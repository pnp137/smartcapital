About SmartCapital

SmartCapital provides fast, flexible working capital solutions for India’s MSMEs through our Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) program. We leverage your digital sales to match repayment with your daily cash flow. With deep experience in informal lending and a seamless digital collection ecosystem, we help businesses grow without the stress of fixed EMIs.

Loan Offering

We offer short-term advances based on your future digital or card sales. You repay a fixed percentage of your daily sales — automatically and effortlessly.

Feature Details
Loan Amount ₹50,000 – ₹1,00,000
Tenure 1 – 4 months
Repayment 10% – 20% of daily digital sales
Top-up Available after 50% – 60% repayment
Repayment Mode POS, Payment Gateway, or NACH
Ideal For Kirana, Restaurants, Petrol Pumps, Pharmacies, Online Sellers
